In short: OLK App does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device with no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics.

Introduction

OLK App ("we", "our", "the app") is a Korean language learning application built for beginners. Your privacy matters to us, and we have designed the app to respect it fully. This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) is collected when you use OLK App.

Information We Do Not Collect

OLK App does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information is collected through the app
  • No location data is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No advertising SDKs or ad networks are integrated

Feedback Feature

OLK App includes an optional "Send Feedback" button that opens your device's default email client (such as Mail or Gmail) to compose an email to olkappassist@gmail.com. This is handled entirely by your email app — OLK App does not send, intercept, or store any email data. Any email you choose to send is governed by your email provider's privacy policy.

Text-to-Speech

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech functionality to pronounce Korean words and phrases. This processing happens entirely on your device. No audio data is sent to any external server.

External Links

The app contains links to our social media pages (Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, Facebook) and our support email. Tapping these links will open external apps or your browser. Once you leave OLK App, you are subject to the privacy policies of those external services.

Children's Privacy

OLK App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Data Storage

All learning content is bundled within the app itself. No data is downloaded from or uploaded to external servers during normal use. The app does not store any user-generated data on your device beyond the app's own installation files.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Effective" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
